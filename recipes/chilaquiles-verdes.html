Ingredients

  • 3 cups salsa verde
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 3 eggs
  • Tortilla chips
  • Cotija cheese
  • Cilantro for garnish
  • Lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Add some oil to a cast iron skillet or saut√© pan.
  2. Cut red onion into half slices and place into bowl with lime juice. Set aside.
  3. Add salsa verde to skillet and heat until hot and bubbling.
  4. Lighlty oil another frying pan and heat until hot. Cooks eggs per preference.
  5. Add half of the onion slices to salsa verde and stir. Remove skillet from heat.
  6. Fill the skillet with tortilla chips and toss until coated.
  7. Add remaining onion slices to top off dish.
  8. Top chilaquiles with eggs, crumbled cotija cheese and cilantro leaves.
